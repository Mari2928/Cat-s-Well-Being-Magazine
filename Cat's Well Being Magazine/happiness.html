Touching

Touching a cat is an effective way to make the cat feel happy, if you do it well. You have to consider the timing and type of touch in order to keep your cat happy. In particular, stroking, rubbing, cutting nails, wiping the body, and giving medication require different considerations. For example, when clipping nails or giving medication, a cat will rarely let you touch unless he or she trusts you. Here are some tips for how to touch cat in a way that he or she loves and that keeps you safe.
