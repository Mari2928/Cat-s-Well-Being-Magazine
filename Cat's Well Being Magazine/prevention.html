Importance of hydration

Cats may live with less water without compromising their physical functionality. But we should understand that the lack of water burdens organs. When cats lived in the wild, they consumed water and food together when they ate a captured creature. Dry cat food may be convenient for humans to feed cats, but cats lose body fluid due to digestion of dry food. Also, it is said that cats cannot absorb the water they drink into their bodies as effectively as humans. Here, we will discuss the best hydration method for cats.

Do annual checkup

Regular health checks are the basis for cat disease prevention. By checking that your cat is young and healthy from time to time, you can keep track of his or her normal blood pressure and many other statistics. If, for example, a cat loses his energy but the vital signs remain normal, you may conclude that it is not due to illness but to stress or due to aging or environmental change. This section describes the items to be checked by checkup, how to use them, and how often the check up should be done.
